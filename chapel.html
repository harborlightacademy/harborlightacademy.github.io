Harbor Light Academy

Chapel

Harbor Light Academy is a nondenominational Christian school that welcomes students and families of all faiths. Each Wednesday, students from preschool through 6th grade gather for Chapel, which serves as the spiritual anchor of our week.

Chapel Leadership

Growing in Faith, Together

Pastor Nathan Webb of Coastal Community Church leads a rotating team of local pastors and school leaders who encourage students to grow in their relationship with Jesus through this time of prayer, music, scripture, and discussion.
