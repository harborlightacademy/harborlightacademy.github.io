At Harbor Light Academy, education will be more than academics — it is the shaping of character, the nurturing of faith, the building of confidence, and the preparation of young hearts and minds for a meaningful future.

As a nonprofit private school, we will rely on the generosity of families, future alumni, and friends who believe in the power of a values-centered education.

When you give, you are not simply making a donation — you are investing in a child's future, strengthening our community, and helping us continue a mission that changes lives every single day.

We are deeply grateful for your generosity, your prayers, and your belief in what we do. Together, we can continue to provide an education that inspires excellence, builds character, and prepares students to make a difference in the world.
