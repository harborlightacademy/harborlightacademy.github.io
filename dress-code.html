Dress Code

Our dress code supports a respectful, focused learning environment while helping students feel confident and comfortable each day.

Guidelines by Category

Tops

  • Solid polo or collared shirts
  • Small logos only (no larger than a quarter)
  • Shirts must be tucked in
  • Plain sweaters may be worn over collared shirts

Bottoms

  • Khaki or navy pants, properly fitted
  • Belts required if pants have loops

Shorts, Skirts & Skorts

  • Must be within 2 inches of the knee
  • No cargo shorts
  • Skirts and jumpers: no shorter than 2 inches above the knee and no longer than mid-calf

Shoes

  • Closed-back, secure shoes only (no Crocs or backless styles)
  • Laces must be tied
  • Athletic shoes required for PE

Socks & Leggings

  • Socks must be worn at all times
  • Tights/leggings must be white or navy

Hair & Appearance

  • Hair should be neat and natural
  • Boys' hair above the collar and no longer than mid-ear
  • Hair kept out of eyes
  • Jewelry and makeup should be simple and age-appropriate
